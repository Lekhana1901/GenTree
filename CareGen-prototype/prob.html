GenLink – Family Health Challenges

Understanding the key problems GenLink aims to solve

Core Problems

Individual Health Management

Most people manage their health individually, without considering the medical history of their family, missing important hereditary patterns.

Scattered Health Data

Health information is often scattered across different family members and generations, making it difficult to track overall family health.

Late Detection of Hereditary Diseases

Families often discover genetic or hereditary conditions too late due to lack of organized records and proactive analysis.

Lack of Interactive Visualization

There is no simple, interactive method to view and analyze shared health patterns across family generations effectively.

Privacy Concerns

Health data is sensitive, and sharing information among family members or platforms can compromise privacy if not properly secured.

Common Health Problems Across Families

Diabetes

One of the most common hereditary diseases. Poor lifestyle habits combined with genetics make it a long-term challenge for many families.

Heart Disease

High blood pressure, cholesterol, and lifestyle issues often run in families, making early awareness crucial for prevention.

Hypertension

Often unnoticed until it causes complications, hypertension tends to appear across generations without early tracking.

Obesity

Genetic predisposition, combined with dietary habits, contributes to obesity that affects both children and adults in families.

Cancer

Several cancers have hereditary tendencies. Family history awareness can help in early detection and timely treatment.

Thyroid Disorders

Thyroid imbalances frequently affect multiple family members, especially women, and need consistent monitoring.

Respiratory Issues

Asthma and related breathing conditions often pass genetically, worsening with pollution or allergens in families.

Mental Health Disorders

Conditions like depression, anxiety, and bipolar disorder can have both genetic and environmental causes within families.
